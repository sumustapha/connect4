How to Play
×
  1. Players must connect 4 of the same colored discs in a row to win.
  2. Only one piece is played at a time.
  3. The board is filled from the bottom to the top
  4. Players can be on the offensive or defensive.
  5. The game ends when there is a 4-in-a-row or a stalemate.
  6. The game ends when 15secs passes when a move is not made and the score is incremented in favour of the opponent.
  7. Pressing the restart button clears the score of both players.
